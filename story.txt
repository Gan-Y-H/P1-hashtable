There is a crowd of todpoles in a pond. The todpoles have big heads with long tails, and they live in the pond happily.
They don't look like the frogs whose egg they hatched from. They are worried because they cannot find their mom.


// middle of the story
Although these todpoles worried about their mom, they forgot about it when they grew up. They would like to play with shark. 
They believe they are children of the shark.
